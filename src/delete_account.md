# Deleting your account

If for any reason you wish to have all account information removed from our servers, we've made the process straightforward and secure. We respect your decision and have implemented features that allow for the **complete deletion of your account and all related data**.

Please remember, this is a permanent action and cannot be undone.

## How to Delete Your Account
We value your privacy and data security. If you wish to remove your account and all associated data from Stressy, you can easily do so by following the steps below.

##### Step 1: Open the App
Start by launching the Stressy on your device.

##### Step 2: Navigate to your Profile
From the main menu go to the 'Profile' tab.

##### Step 3: Locate the "More Settings" Section
In the 'Profile' menu, scroll down to the section labeled 'More Settings.'

##### Step 4: Click on "Delete Account"
Tap the 'Delete Account' button located under the 'More settings' section.

##### Step 5: Confirm Your Action
You'll receive a prompt asking if you're sure you want to delete your account. Confirm your action to proceed. Please note that this action is irreversible.

##### Step 6: Complete the Deletion Process
Follow any additional instructions or verification steps to finalize the account deletion process. After this, all your account data will be permanently removed from our servers.

### Additional Information

* Reversibility: Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.
* Timeframe: The deletion process may take up to 48 hours to completely remove all your data from our servers.
